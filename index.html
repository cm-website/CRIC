App Description

CRIC (Cardiovascular Risk Index Calculator) is a comprehensive tool designed to provide users with valuable insights into their cardiovascular health. With its user-friendly interface, CRIC allows individuals to estimate their risk for cardiovascular disease, offering a convenient way to stay informed about their heart health. The app is equipped with the latest updates in cardiology, ensuring that users have access to the most current information available. Whether you're looking to monitor your heart health or simply stay informed, CRIC is an essential tool for anyone interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Please note that CRIC provides estimates of cardiovascular disease risk for informational purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
